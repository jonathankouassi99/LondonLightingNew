ABOUT US

LONDON LIGHTING

London Lighting, based in East London, is a leading lighting production company known for illuminating music videos, commercials, and fashion shoots across the UK. We've worked with top artists like Ed Sheeran and brands like Creed, combining special effects and cutting-edge technology to set ourselves apart. Our team has rapidly expanded, offering a blend of creativity and technical expertise, making us a go-to for high-impact, visually stunning projects nationwide. We're dedicated to pushing creative boundaries.
